How nice of you to want to know more about me

My name is Muhammad Alif Farchan. Graduated with International Relations degree back in 2023, worked as video editor before and now I’m the branding guy at a Jakarta & London based consulting firm.

Since my significant other introduced me to film photography around 2024, I’ve been using it as an escapism from this capitalistic hell hole that has been draining my sanity every time I clock in.
